Hi, I'm Grace O'Brien!

Creativity has always been a part of my life - from graphic design to photography to painting to music, there's always been an innate need to create.

Part of that creativity has led to a curiousity with logic. It gives me great joy to problem solve and search for the connecting patterns in our world.

Previously, in 2015 I completed a Bachelor's in Intercultural Studies: International Development - and a highlight of my studies was the opportunity I had to learn the basics of 3 different languages - Mandarin, Hindi, and Arabic.

Beginning of the summer of 2017, I told myself if I could find a line of work that combined those three together, creativity, logic, and languages, that I would be satisfied. It was that same summer that I picked up web design on the side as a hobby. For the first time I learned about Javascript and that was when it clicked. Right there, I found all three things that I had been looking for, and immediately I was hooked. I delved deeper and deeper, only to get absolutely hooked. The more I got into the programming side of web development, the more I realized that this is what I wanted to do.

A year after self study and I'm now back in school with the goal to graduate with a BS in Computer Science. My passion and goal is to utilize this momentum to become a part of the movement using technology to make this world a better place through creative and practical innovation.
